About this site

Welcome!

This is the personal website of Stefano Gombi, a pretty cool guy from Italy.

It's a place where I'm free to express my opinions and to suggests interesting resources, often regarding software design and production.

This site serves also as an exercise, as it forces me to improve myself under several aspects:

  • to put my thoughts in order, instead of leaving them the messy chaos they usually are;
  • to improve my written english (don't laugh) by trying to write correctly and to learn new words;
  • to struggle against my perfectionism, who would have required years of work on little details, making this site never see the light.

Opinions expressed are my own and they must not be taken too seriously. I hope to gain much more experience in life: probably I'll learn more about what I'm writing today, and maybe my opinions will be different in the future. Things will change.

Even technically, this site is an expression of my opinions and an exercise. It's built to be simple, to give a sense of order, and to be easily understandable. Also, I've forced myself to publish it when it was completely under construction, with not even posts' pagination, or this page; all this in order to demonstrate to myself that no-one would have laughed at me for not having much time to work on this. I hope that, at the time you're reading this text, things look more complete.

About me

I like to garden, read, get lost in nature. Wow, that sounds boring once written.
